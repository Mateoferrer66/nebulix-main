## Mailing list

Our newsletter feature is currently powered by Netlify edge functions and is compatible exclusively with Mailchimp. Set up your newsletter by providing your Mailchimp credentials in the .env file

```.env
NEWSLETTER_PROVIDER=mailchimp
MAILCHIMP_API_KEY=XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX-us21
MAILCHIMP_SERVER_PREFIX=us21	
MAILCHIMP_LIST_ID=XXXXXXXXX
```

You can edit the **Join Us** text in your site settings `SETTINGS > ABOUT`
