**New License Option**

Want to remove attribution from your StarFunnel website? You can now purchase a license that's identical to the previous one but without the requirement to display it in your footer.


[Check out the StarFunnel Theme](https://starfunnel.unfolding.io/)


**Subdomains Included**

With one license, you can remove attribution for 1 main domain and up to 3 subdomains. For instance: `www.my-domain.com`, `shop.my-domain.com`, `blog.my-domain.com`, and `menu.my-domain.com`.
